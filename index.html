Recommendations

Here's where you should stop. Vermont is well known for their outdoor sports and scenery, but they also have some small gems that you can find. The people are very kind and are open to all walks to life. As long as you aren't creating a fuss, you're welcome!

Vermont Farmer's Market
Tons of fresh produce and local artisanal crafts
Hackett's Apple Orchard
Small family owned apple orchard with delicious donuts and cider
Wally's Place
Local deli featuring local veggies and fresh bagels
Tiny Thai Restaurant
Authentic Thai food found in the small town of Winooski
